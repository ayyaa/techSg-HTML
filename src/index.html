ATRACTING MORE TALENT

A GROWING ECO SYSTEM

In two years beetween 2015 and 2017, the numbers of startups at launchpad has grown from 216 to 361. More facilitatore are also on hand to nurture these young companies and help them unlock their true potential. Launchpad is home to 64 such facilitators, an increase from 41 in 2015. And this numbers are only growing.

INNOVATION HOTBED

Fresh ideas are constantly making their way to launchpad, creating a dynamic ecosystem that buzzes activity. Many of the firms at launchpad have only entered within the last 15 months, and each new entry brings with in an enthusiansm and passion that's highly contagious.

YOUNG AND RARING TO GO

It's no secret Launchpad is home to the Next Big idea. Most firms here are less than two years old, witch means there are plenty of fresh ideas bounching arounds. There's also an athmosphere of innovation, creativity and urgency that's hard to find elsewhere.
